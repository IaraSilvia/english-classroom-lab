> Creative resources for meaningful English learning.

Welcome to **English Classroom Lab**, a repository dedicated to sharing classroom activities, worksheets, lesson ideas and teaching resources designed for English language learning.

The materials published here were developed from real classroom experiences and aim to promote communication, creativity and student engagement.

---

## About

This project combines my background in:

- 🎓 B.A. in Portuguese/English Language (Uni-FACEF, 2009)
- 💻 Undergraduate student in Information Technology
- ✅ Quality Assurance (QA)
- 🤖 Artificial Intelligence applied as a support tool for educational content development

Technology and AI were used to improve organization, revision and productivity throughout the creation process. However, every activity was planned, reviewed and adapted according to pedagogical objectives and real classroom practice.
