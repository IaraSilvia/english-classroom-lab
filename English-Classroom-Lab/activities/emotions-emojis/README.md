# 😊 Emotions & Emojis

An engaging classroom activity designed to help English learners identify, express and discuss emotions through creativity and communication.

---

## Overview

**Emotions & Emojis** is a printable worksheet developed for beginner English learners (A1–A2) as part of an English Basic Course.

Students are invited to create their own emojis representing different feelings and emotions. More than a vocabulary exercise, the activity encourages learners to communicate personal experiences, practice speaking and build empathy within the classroom.

During its classroom application, this activity generated laughter, meaningful conversations and emotional connections, showing that language learning becomes more effective when students feel comfortable expressing themselves.

---

## Learning Objectives

- Expand vocabulary related to feelings and emotions.
- Develop speaking and communication skills.
- Encourage creativity and self-expression.
- Promote empathy and active listening.
- Foster interaction among classmates.

---

## Target Audience

- Beginner English learners (A1–A2)
- Teenagers
- Adults
- Language schools
- Private English lessons

---

## Skills Practiced

- Vocabulary
- Speaking
- Listening
- Creativity
- Communication
- Social interaction

---

## Materials

- Printed worksheet
- Colored pencils, markers or crayons
- Optional: scissors and glue

---

## Estimated Time

**20–50 minutes**

The activity can be adapted according to the class size and learning objectives.

---

## Classroom Experience

This worksheet was developed from a real classroom experience.

Although initially proposed as a fun drawing activity, students naturally began sharing personal stories and discussing how they felt in different situations. The classroom became a welcoming environment where humor, empathy and meaningful communication emerged spontaneously.

This experience reinforced an important teaching principle:

> Language learning is not only about grammar and vocabulary. It is also about creating opportunities for authentic human connection.
